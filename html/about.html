Our Story

Founded in 2009, Interconnecx has been at the forefront of the hosting industry for over 15 years. What started as a small operation in Detroit, Michigan has grown into a trusted infrastructure provider serving thousands of businesses across the globe.

We specialize in providing enterprise-class dedicated servers, virtual private servers, and colocation services from our state-of-the-art Tier 3 data centers. Our commitment to excellence has made us a preferred partner for businesses that demand reliability, performance, and exceptional support.

Today, we operate multiple data centers across the United States, including facilities in Detroit, Kansas City, Cleveland, and Dallas, ensuring low latency and high availability for our clients nationwide.
