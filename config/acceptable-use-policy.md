## 1. Purpose

## 2. Prohibited Activities

You may **NOT** use our services for any of the following:

### 2.1 Illegal Activities
- Any activity that violates local, state, national, or international law
- Distribution of pirated software, movies, music, or other copyrighted materials
- Hacking, cracking, or unauthorized access to computer systems
- Distribution of viruses, malware, or other malicious software
- Phishing, identity theft, or fraud

### 2.2 Spam and Unsolicited Communications
- Sending unsolicited bulk email (spam)
- Operating email lists without proper opt-in procedures
- Harvesting email addresses from websites or other sources
- Operating open mail relays or proxies

### 2.3 Network Abuse
- Port scanning or security scanning without prior authorization
- Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks
- Interference with service to any user, host, or network
- Forging packet headers or other identification information
- Cryptocurrency mining without prior written approval

### 2.4 Offensive Content
- Child pornography or sexual exploitation of minors
- Content that promotes violence, terrorism, or hatred
- Content that infringes on intellectual property rights
- Defamatory, libelous, or threatening material

### 2.5 Resource Abuse
- Excessive CPU usage that impacts other customers
- Excessive bandwidth usage beyond your service plan
- Running services that violate our Terms of Service
- Using our network to attack other networks or systems

### 2.6 Other Prohibited Uses
- Proxy services or VPN services (unless explicitly allowed in your plan)
- Tor exit nodes
- Adult content hosting (unless explicitly allowed in your plan)
- File sharing or torrent tracking (unless explicitly allowed)
- IRC bots or bouncers (unless explicitly allowed)
